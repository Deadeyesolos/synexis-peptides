Product Description

Semax is derived from the ACTH(4–10) sequence and extended for stability. It is supplied as a lyophilised powder for research and in-vitro use only. Not for human administration.

Technical Specifications

  • Purity: ≥98% (HPLC Verified)

Storage Instructions

Store lyophilised peptide at -20°C in a dry, dark environment. Reconstitute with bacteriostatic water for laboratory use. Once reconstituted, store refrigerated at 2°C–8°C and use within 28 days.
