Batch Testing & Quality Assurance

Synexis Peptides is committed to uncompromising transparency. We utilise independent, third-party HPLC testing to verify sequence identity and purities exceeding 99%.

What is a COA?

A Certificate of Analysis (COA) is a verified document provided largely by independent analytical laboratories. It confirms the molecular sequence identity and calculates the percentage purity of the synthesised peptide, ensuring there are no dangerous impurities or degradation products prior to release for research use.

Why Batch Testing Matters

In-vitro research demands strict structural consistency to yield reproducible, accurate data. Skipping rigorous quality assurance limits experimental reliability. We maintain strict batch testing protocols via High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Mass Spectrometry (MS) to protect the integrity of your research data.
